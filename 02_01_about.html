who we are

Mzuka Health Solutions Limited is one of the leading professional suppliers &distributors of human healthcare equipments, Surgical materials and non pharmaceutical items in Kenya and East Africa. Our product range widely covers; Surgical equipment, X-ray machines, Lab devices, Patient Monitors, ECG's, Ultrasound Scanners, Dental Equipments, Hospital Furnitures and many more.

Established in 2010, as one of Kenyas professional medical equipment supply companies, we specialize majorly in marketing & distribution of various hospital equipments (apparatus), medical disposable items and household medical appliances.

Our efficient and effective groups of professional technical and sales staff are able to provide you complete range of service at any time.

Please do not hesitate to contact us if you are interested in any of our products. We are looking forward to establishing business relationships with different types of companies worldwide.
